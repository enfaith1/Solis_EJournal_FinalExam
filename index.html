INDUSTRY TOUR

Mindanao Media Hub PTV

Speakers: Clyde Allan Pudpu & Rannie Claire Cardino

Background

PTV News is a television broadcasting network that delivers news and public information to viewers. It operates news production facilities, studios, and technical systems that enable the delivery of live and recorded broadcasts.

Schedule

  1. 01
    Arrival: After arriving at Mindanao Media Hub, we were warmly greeted by the staff members.
  2. 02
    Tour #1: Guided tour of the NewsRoom and Green Screen Studio, including a demonstration of chroma key technology and photo opportunities.
  3. 03
    Demonstration: Observation of a live news broadcast and real-time production processes, followed by a Q&A session.
  4. 04
    Tour #2: Tour of the Control Room and other operational areas, including an overview of broadcasting equipment and facilities.
  5. 05
    Exit: Return to the bus and depart from the facility.

Summary

The tour showcased many aspects of operating a live broadcasting news station. We saw various parts of the station including the green screen room, control room, servers, and more. It demonstrated live news performance alongside technical aspects like live video editing, teleprompters, and lighting systems.

Technologies Observed

  • GS
    Green Screen Technology (Chroma Keying)

    Used to replace the green background with virtual news sets and graphics.

  • CR
    Control Room Systems

    Used for managing cameras, video switching, audio, lighting, and live broadcasts.

  • BS
    Broadcast Servers

    Store and manage video content, graphics, and program materials.

  • VE
    Video Editing Systems

    Enable real-time editing and switching of content during live broadcasts.

  • TP
    Teleprompter Systems

    Allow news presenters to read scripts while maintaining eye contact with the camera.

  • MW
    Microwave Transmission Technology

    Used for transmitting audio and video signals between locations and broadcasting facilities.

SEMINAR

UIC Fr. Selga Campus

Speakers: Ronald Pelayo Maslog, Lemuel Abellana & Kimberly Cabagti

Breakdown

  1. 01
    Introduction of Blockchain & Traditional vs. Decentralized Blockchains: The seminar started with a core discussion defining blockchain.
  2. 02
    Application of Blockchain: The seminar then moved into real-world use cases of how the blockchain is used.
  3. 03
    Integration of Blockchain: Lastly, it was explained how blockchain architectures are practically implemented.
  4. 04
    Activity: To close off, we had a presentation activity that involved creating a prototype utilizing blockchain technology concepts.
  5. 05
    Client-Facing Mindset Seminar: After the blockchain presentation, Ms. Kimberly gave us the final seminar on how to interact effectively with clients.

Summary

The seminar heavily focused on blockchain, a technology that rivals centralized data repositories by ensuring everyone can see a shared set of data. The activity involved a group task where students designed a simple prototype using blockchain technology as a core feature. Lastly, Ms. Kimberly shared her professional insights on how to manage clients, properly engage in conversation, and listen actively.

Technologies Discussed

    Blockchain

    This was the highlight topic discussed extensively by speakers Sir Ronald Maslog and Sir Lemuel Abellana.

  • CC
