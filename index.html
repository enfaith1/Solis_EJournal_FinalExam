INDUSTRY TOUR

Mindanao Media Hub PTV

Speakers: Clyde Allan Pudpu & Rannie Claire Cardino

Background

PTV News is a TV network that shares news and public updates. It has studios and equipment for live and recorded broadcasts. The hub was a shrine until 2017. The building was finished in 2020 and stands on about one hectare of land.

Building Layout

  • 1F

    Broadcasting Room — where news programs are aired.

  • 2F

    Production Room — where shows are prepared and produced.

  • 3F

    Server Room — where data and broadcast files are stored.

Itinerary

  1. 08:30
    Call Time: We met and prepared for the trip.
  2. 09:00
    Travel: We traveled to Mindanao Media Hub Davao from 9:00 to 9:20 AM.
  3. 09:30
    Tour: We toured the facility from 9:30 to 11:20 AM.

Schedule

  1. 01
    Arrival: We arrived at Mindanao Media Hub around 9:20 AM and were welcomed by the staff.
  2. 02
    Tour #1: We visited the newsroom and green screen studio and saw how chroma key works.
  3. 03
    Demonstration: We watched a live news segment and how the team produces it, then asked questions.
  4. 04
    Tour #2: We toured the control room and other work areas and saw broadcast equipment.
  5. 05
    Exit: We went back to the bus and left the site.

Summary

The tour showed how a live news station works across three floors. We saw the broadcasting, production, and server areas, plus the green screen room and control room. We also learned about live editing, teleprompters, and studio lighting.

Technologies Observed

  • GS
    Green Screen Technology (Chroma Keying)

    Removes the green backdrop and adds virtual sets or graphics.

  • CR
    Control Room Systems

    Runs cameras, audio, lighting, and live video switching.

  • BS
    Broadcast Servers

    Stores videos, graphics, and show files.

  • VE
    Video Editing Systems

    Edits and switches footage during live shows.

  • TP
    Teleprompter Systems

    Shows the script so anchors can read while facing the camera.

  • MW
    Microwave Transmission Technology

    Sends audio and video signals between sites and the station.

SEMINAR

UIC Fr. Selga Campus

Speakers: Ronald Pelayo Maslog, Lemuel Abellana & Kimberly Cabagti

Breakdown

  1. 01
    Introduction of Blockchain & Traditional vs. Decentralized Blockchains: The talk began with what blockchain is and how it differs from traditional systems.
  2. 02
    Application of Blockchain: Next, speakers shared real uses of blockchain.
  3. 03
    Integration of Blockchain: They also explained how blockchain is built and used in practice.
  4. 04
    Activity: We ended with a group activity to design a simple blockchain-based prototype.
  5. 05
    Client-Facing Mindset Seminar: Ms. Kimberly closed with tips on working well with clients.

Summary

The seminar focused on blockchain, which lets people share the same data without one central owner. We also did a group task to plan a prototype using blockchain ideas. Ms. Kimberly shared advice on client talks, good communication, and active listening.
